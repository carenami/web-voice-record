Key Word Recording

Greetings! We are asking for friends and family to help us in building an automatic key phrase detector that can detect the phrase "help! help! help!". We plan to use this detector in a device that will allow a person to contact a guardian or a caregiver by simply using voice command.
For this purpose, the Carenami team is hoping to gather spoken words "help!" and "help me" from several hundred people to create a simple speech command classifier.
